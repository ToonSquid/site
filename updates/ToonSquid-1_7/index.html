ToonSquid 1.7

02 December 2022


ToonSquid 1.7 is out now and it comes with many exciting improvements. Here are the most important ones:

  • Copy, cut and paste selection areas
  • Fill reference layers
  • Perspective and warp scaling modes
  • Image sequence import
  • Added a setting to move between drawings instead of frames with the forward / backward buttons
  • Transparency lock for animation layers
  • Stability improvements
  • Bug fixes
